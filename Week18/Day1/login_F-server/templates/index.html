Welcome to Pizza Outside-In (POI)

What is your innovation strategy? Outside-in or Inside-out?

Definitions:

  • Inside-Out: an approach that is conducted around the belief that an organization's capabilities and inner strengths are more valuable than outside influences, and will be the key to success.
  • Outside-In: an approach that is conducted by the thought process that customer value creation, customer insights, and customer experiences are the keys to success.

When it came to innovation, Steve Jobs was very opinionated in regard to receiving customer feedback through the outside-in mentality. He has stated: “It's really hard to design products by focus groups. A lot of times, people don't know what they want until you show it to them.”

We in POI established that eating to, collecting, and carrying pizza could be a lot easier, and it also needed to look good while doing it. Our product is not a result of round-table discussions or basic wants/needs surveys that companies typically conduct.

That's why we implementing the cutting edge technology to pizza manufacturing by using Outside-In approach!
